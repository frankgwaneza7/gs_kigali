About Us

Uburezi bw'u Rwanda, n' inkingi y'iterambere k' u Rwanda ndetse no ku mahanga.Abana b' igihugu bose bakwiye amahirwe angana yaba abakobwa ndetse n' abahungu hatitawe ku mikoro ya buri umwe.

The Parliament has enacted laws related to education, which aim to ensure access, equity, and quality at all levels. These laws establish the structure of the education system (primary, secondary, higher education, TVET), mandatory schooling, and the roles of various stakeholders.
